Given a polynomial $P(x)$ of degree $n$, the task is to calculate coefficients of $Q(x) = P(x + a)$. To do that, calculate: 
\begin{align*}
U_k &= P_{n-k} (n - k)! \\
V_k &= \frac{a^k}{k!} \\
G &= (U \ast V) \\
Q_k &= \frac{G_k}{k!}
\end{align*}
